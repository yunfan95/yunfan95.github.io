Research

Project I: LEO satellie localization
From 2021-now, we are working on localization of miniature RF trackers using low earth orbit (LEO) satellites.

  • Global Localization of Energy-Constrained Miniature RF Emitters using Low Earth Orbit Satellites
    Yunfan Wang, Steve Young, Demba Komma, Jaechan Lim, Zhen Feng, Zichen Fan, Chien-wei Tseng, Hun-Seok Kim, and David Blaauw
    in 21th ACM Conference on Embedded Networked Sensor Systems (Sensys), 2023.

Project II: Tera-Hertz (THz) circuits and systems
From 2018 to 2021, we collaborated with Professor Wenhua Chen, Dr. Xiaofan Chen, Xingcun Li, Long Chen, and Shuyang Li on designing THz oscillator, power amplifier, antenna, and non-reciprocal components using the SiGe BiCMOS process.

  • Highly Efficient Terahertz Beam-Steerable Integrated Radiator Based on Tunable Boundary Conditions
    Yunfan Wang, Wenhua Chen, Xingcun Li, Jiaxian Chen, Long Chen, Fei Huang, Shuyang Li, and ZhaoZhuo Wang
    in Journal of Solid-state Circuit (JSSC) (invited paper), 2022.

  • 305-325 GHz Non-Reciprocal Isolator Based on Peak-Control Gain-boosting Magnetless Non-reciprocal Metamaterials
    Yunfan Wang, Wenhua Chen, Xingcun Li, Shuyang Li, and Peigen Zhou
    in Symposium on Radio Frequency Integrated Circuit (RFIC), 2021.

  • 300-335 GHz Highly Efficient Beam-Steerable Radiator Based on Tunable Boundary Conditions
    Yunfan Wang, Wenhua Chen, Xingcun Li, Jiaxin Chen, Long Chen, and Shuyang Li
    in Symposium on Radio Frequency Integrated Circuit (RFIC), 2021.

  • A 210-GHz Magnetless Nonreciprocal Isolator in 130-nm SiGe Bicmos Based on Resistor-free Unidirectional Ring Resonators
    Yunfan Wang, Wenhua Chen, and Xingcun Li
    in Microwave and Wireless Component Letter (MWCL), 2020.

  • Highly Linear and Magnetless Isolator Based on Weakly Coupled Nonreciprocal Metamaterials
    Yunfan Wang, Wenhua Chen, and Xiaofan Chen
    in Transaction on Microwave Theory and Technique (TMTT), 2019.
